Services

This chatbot can diagnose the disease and provide basic details about the disease before consulting a doctor. The system provides text assistance you can communicate with bot like user friendly Bot will provides which type of disease you have based on user symptoms and appeared doctor details respective to user disease and also provides food suggestion that means which type of food you have to take and if the problem is much bigger than it will suggest you to book an appointment of a dedicated doctor and allows you to connect to doctor via appointment and it can guide you to hospital or help with the address by the help of Google maps.

DOCTORS

Booking Appointments: Chatbots can be integrated with hospital management systems to allow users to book appointments with a doctor or specialist. The bot can provide information on available dates and times, and help the user schedule an appointment.

SUGGESTING MEDICINE

Based on the user's symptoms, the chatbot can suggest appropriate medicine and dosage. The bot can also provide information on any side effects and precautions to take when using the medication

ONLINE CONSULTATION

Chatbots can be programmed to provide basic medical advice and triage. In the case of more serious medical issues, the bot can connect the user with a licensed healthcare professional for an online consultation.

MEDICINE DELIVERY

Chatbots can be integrated with online pharmacies to allow for convenient medicine delivery. The user can place an order for medicine and have it delivered to their doorstep
